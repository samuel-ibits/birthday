Happy Birthday, My Special One

Today is all about you… and everything that makes you beautiful

↓

“I remember asking you about the past year…
and you said it has been a year of many tears.

But even through that, you are still here… still standing… still shining.
That says everything about your strength.”

“You said you feel happy and grateful to God…
and honestly, that’s one of the things I admire most about you.

Your heart… your faith… your quiet strength…
it’s rare, and it’s beautiful.”

“When I asked about your deepest prayers,
you said… that’s between you and God.

And I respect that deeply.

But I hope—truly—that every silent prayer,
every hidden hope,
and every tear you never explained…

turns into something beautiful for you this year.”

“You are stronger than you know,
more beautiful than you realize,
and more special than words can fully capture.

There’s something about you…
your presence, your energy, your soul…
it just stays with me.

And I’m really grateful to have you in my life.”

“On your birthday, I wish you peace that stays,
joy that overflows,
love that feels safe,
and a year that gives back to you everything you’ve given to others.

You deserve soft days, loud laughter,
and moments that remind you how amazing you truly are.”

Cheers to greatness, Nwamaka 🥂
